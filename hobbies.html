Short films

I have also done some filming, on this film I aimed to use the spectacled caiman to show to the viewer a sense of the daily live in the Colombian's eastern flatlands that we call the Llanos. I named the film El llano a los ojos de una babilla which means The llanos by the eyes of a spectacled caiman. I participated with this film on the 2016 competition filminutos del Día Mundial de la Diversidad Biológica directed by the Humbolt institution and I won in the amateur category.
