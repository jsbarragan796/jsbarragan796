Academic interests

Water quality

One of my biggest intrests on the environmental engineering field is the water quality modeling, One of the well-known model for this is call Qual2K. This is a One dimensional river and stream water quality model, that is able to model a horizontal and vertical well-mixed channel. I have use this tool in several courses an I believe a have a pretty good understanding of it. Once you have a calibrated model of a particular river, stream or creek you will be able to answer questions like what if a factory discharge certain flow with known characteristics (Oxygen dissolve, nutrients, temperature, DOB ... ) it is possible to estimate the impact downstream.
One thing that drives me crazy about it is that is major component is written in Fortran that makes me wonder if there is room for improvements, what definitely needs a improvement is the display of the results, it is not user friendly.

Short films

I have also done some filming, on this film I aimed to use the spectacled caiman to show to the viewer a sense of the daily live in the Colombian's eastern flatlands that we call the Llanos. I named the film El llano a los ojos de una babilla which means The llanos by the eyes of a spectacled caiman. I participated with this film on the 2016 competition filminutos del Día Mundial de la Diversidad Biológica directed by the Humbolt institution and I won in the amateur category.
