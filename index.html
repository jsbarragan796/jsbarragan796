Academic interests

Water quality

One of my biggest interests on the environmental engineering field is the water quality modeling, One of the well-known model for this is call Qual2K. This is a One dimensional river and stream water quality model, that is able to model a horizontal and vertical well-mixed channel. I have use this tool in several courses an I believe a have a pretty good understanding of it. Once you have a calibrated model of a particular river, stream or creek you will be able to answer questions like what if a factory discharge certain flow with known characteristics (Oxygen dissolve, nutrients, temperature, DOB ... ) it is possible to estimate the impact downstream.
One thing that drives me crazy about it is that is major component is written in Fortran that makes me wonder if there is room for improvements, what definitely needs a improvement is the display of the results, it is not user friendly.

Dron Mapping

Another field inside environmental engineering that interests me is the Geographic information system (GIS). It is about collecting and displaying the spacial information of a given place. This has a huge kinds of applications in many fields,but in the environmental engineering applications are focus on prevention of disasters and hydrology.
